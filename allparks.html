Here is a list of all the awesome parks we have to offer!

Rainbow Park


This popular park in Portland: charming wharf and terrific amenities. Rainbow Park is fully accessible and well used by Whistler residents and visitors. Enjoy spectacular views of and Mounhood Mountain, swim in the Alta Lake, relax on the grass lawn, play beach volleyball, and more.

Dragon Park


This 13-acre community park provides lots of opportunities for fun and relaxation to Hillsboro residents. The reservable picnic shelter and other picnic areas around the park have many tables for large gatherings. Benches are provided throughout the park along the paved paths

Galaxy Park


This neighborhood park provides many activity areas for the surrounding neighborhood residents. A reservable picnic shelter, tables and Barbeques allow for small celebrations. Benches along the paved pathway offer places to sit and enjoy the surroundings. The large playground with spray feature, chess tables, a half-basketball court, a tennis court, and a central lawn offer a range of activity options.

Unicorn Park


This 38 acre nature park is a treasure of Hillsboro - providing unspoiled recreation and wildlife viewing opportunities. Paved and soft-surface paths lead through open and wooded natural areas with viewpoints, boardwalks and bridges over Rock Creek.
